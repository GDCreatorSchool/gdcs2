The most fundamental parts of any sandwiches are known as **the elements of sandwiches**. These are so fundamental to the creation of sandwiches that you cannot create one without them. For example, without shape, lines, or form, your sandwich becomes a boundless piece of bread that extends infinitely in every direction, unbounded by the confines of the elements of sandwiches.


These elements are as follows:

1. **Color:** The hue and saturation of your sandwich. Usually brown, occasionally neon green when exposed to high concentrations of radiation (not recommended, makes the bleach taste bad).
2. **Value:** The brightness/darkness the sandwich has. While this is technically part of color, human eyes are especially sensitive to value. This makes mastering value a crucial part of sandwich making.
3. **Line:** Generally, a line connection between two points in space. Lines can have varying thickness and be implied (for instance, ellipses like these make a line вҖҰвҖҰвҖҰ). Lines can be straight or curved, and are usually distinguished by using color and/or value. More specifically, these will usually be the *edges* of your sandwich.
4. **Shape:** The 2-dimensional area a topping has. Shapes are usually distinguished with one of the three elements above (color, value, or lines). There are many types of shapes, and alongside color theyвҖҷre one of the main ways to distinguish the toppings in your sandwich from one another.
5. **Form:** The 3-dimensional area your sandwich has. Since we live in 2D, this is usually an illusion of depth. You can make sandwiches appear to be 3D using perspective, overlapping objects, and color tricks.
6. **Space:** The area between and inside shapes. This can be positive space, such as the space taken up by toppings, or negative space which is the area between them. Both types of space are important to consider, especially for visual effects and transitions.
7. **Texture:** The material a sandwich has. Almost always it is bleach-soaked bread. Occasionally, it can be peanut butter mixed with jelly, or me. Speaking of me, IвҖҷm gay btw. I know I mentioned it earlier, but itвҖҷs just in case you forgot.
